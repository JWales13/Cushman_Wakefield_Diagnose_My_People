Location

Location is a major concern for your people. What factors are most important to them? We will use this information to compile information on Parking, Exposure, Transit and Drive Time.

Questions Included:

  • What is your average commute time?
  • How do you currently commute to work?
  • How do you prefer to commute to work?
  • How satisfied are you with parking arrangements at your current work setting?
  • If a partial “work from home” policy was implemented, would you see yourself using this as an alternative working style?

Amenities

Which amenities are most important for your employees? Do they want a building close to restaurants, entertainment, or free parking? Are they interested in an on-site coffee shop or fitness center? We will use this data to report what amenities they want geographically, internally, or in an office building.

Questions Included:

  • Please rate how often you use your current building’s amenities, such as a fitness center or lounge area, in an average week.
  • If a ‘3rd work space’ was available in your building, such as a coffee shop, lounge or common space, how often do you think you would use it in a week?
  • Please rate the following amenities you would like to have in an ideal location: (1 being no preference, 5 being highly preferable): Food and Entertainment, Conference Room, Green Space, Shower/Locker Room, Fitness Center, Parking, Public Transit.

Brand

How do people perceive your brand? Diagnose the impact that your space has on your company culture and image.

Questions Included:

  • How do you perceive our company’s stance on environmental stewardship and sustainability?
  • How well do you feel our current facility reflects our company’s brand and mission statement?
  • What kind of impression do you feel our current work space has on our customers?
  • How would you rate our company’s current public exposure?
  • How important is it for our business to have a public face?
  • How impactful would a new location be to our companies public face?
  • How does our current space compare to other office spaces you have worked in or visited?

Recruitment and Retention

The workspace is now a part of the compensation model for most organizations. Diagnose your people's preferences for culture, well-being, and how your space fosters collaboration.

Questions Included:

  • Please rate your current work areas access to daylight and natural views
  • How much impact does your location have on your workplace satisfaction?
  • How well does your current work environment provide the space to meet you and your teams needs?
  • Where are you working during an average work week?
