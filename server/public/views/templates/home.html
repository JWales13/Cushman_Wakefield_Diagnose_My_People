Our Mission

The typical company that leases or owns real estate spends, on average, about 3%-5% of its annual operating budget on real estate costs. However, anywhere from 50% - 90% of that same operating budget gets spent on recruiting, training, compensating, and retaining top talent. We believe a real estate strategy that does not take into account the people factor is a strategy that is destined to fall short of the operational goals of the organization.


What It Is

The Diagnose My People Tool is the quick and easy way to help facility decision-makers make data-driven decisions supported by analyzing the relationship between your workplace and your workforce. This integrated survey is founded upon workplace best practices across fortune 500 companies, architectural firms, and leading workplace wellness programs. It is deliberately customizable so that a manger can easily curate, administer, and target the metrics that matter the most his or her organization.

What You Get

The Diagnose My People Tool will compile all of the survey responses received in the designated survey period of time, aggregate the responses, and automatically populate your dashboard. You will receive a notification that your dashboard is ready for review in 1-2 weeks once the designated survey period has concluded.
