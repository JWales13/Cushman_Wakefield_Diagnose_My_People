What It Is

The Diagnose My People Tool is the quick and easy way to help facility decision-makers make data-driven choices. This is supported by analyzing the relationship between your workplace and your workforce. This integrated survey is founded upon workplace best practices across fortune 500 companies, architectural firms, and leading workplace wellness programs. It is deliberately customizable so that a manger can easily curate, administer, and target the metrics that matter the most to his or her organization.

How It Works
  • Step 1. Create a company profile and customize your Diagnose My People Tool.
  • Step 2. Distribute the unique URL to your employees.
  • Step 3. Cushman & Wakefield will follow up to review your custom-tailored dashboard

What You Get

The Diagnose My People Tool will compile all of the employee responses received in the alotted period of time, aggregate the responses, and automatically populate your personal dashboard. You will receive a notification that your dashboard is ready for review in 1-2 weeks once all of your people have been diagnosed.
